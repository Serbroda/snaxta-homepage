How it works

1

Create a Snaxta account

Download the Snaxta app and create an account. Remember your username.

2

Send a claim request via email

Send an email to claim@snaxta.com with your username, the restaurant name and proof of ownership (e.g. business registration, Google Business screenshot or photo of your menu with stamp).

3

Verification

We'll review your request and get back to you within a few days.

4

Manage your profile

Once verified, you can manage your description, website, menu and more.
